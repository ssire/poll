The 'Order' document
====================

The 'Order' document instructs the Poll application to generate a customized questionnaire

The 'Order' document is to be POSTed to the /orders URL

Storage
-------

The 'Order' documents are stored inside the '/db/sites/poll/forms/{name}/{YYYY}/{MM}' collection

where 

{name} is the questionnaire name
{YYYY} is the year the 'Order' document has been posted
{MM} is the month the 'Order' document has been posted
